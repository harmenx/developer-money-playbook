# REMOTE_WORK: Developer Money Playbook

A guide for software developers to maximize income, flexibility, and opportunities through remote work. Focused on high-paying roles, niche skills, and location-independent strategies.

---

## 1. Why Remote Work
- Access global companies paying USD/EUR/GBP.  
- Reduce living costs while earning high salaries.  
- Freedom to work from anywhere, enabling side hustles or travel.  

---

## 2. High-Paying Remote Roles
Focus on niches where demand is strong and competition is manageable:  
- **Cloud & DevOps:** AWS, Azure, GCP, CI/CD pipelines.  
- **AI/ML & Data:** GPT integration, computer vision, analytics.  
- **Web & Mobile:** Full-stack frameworks, React, Next.js, Flutter.  
- **Security & Blockchain:** Cybersecurity, smart contracts.  
- **Automation & Scripting:** Productivity tools, workflow automation.  

---

## 3. Where to Find Remote Jobs
- **Job Boards / Platforms:**
  - [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/)  
  - [Remote OK](https://remoteok.io/)  
  - [FlexJobs](https://www.flexjobs.com/)  
  - [AngelList](https://angel.co/jobs) (startups)  
  - Upwork / Toptal (premium freelancing)  

- **Networking:**
  - LinkedIn (target recruiters, post results/projects).  
  - GitHub / Open Source contributions (visibility + credibility).  
  - Tech communities (Discord servers, Slack groups, forums).  

---

## 4. Strategies to Stand Out
- Build a **specialized portfolio** highlighting remote-relevant skills.  
- Include **projects with measurable impact** (e.g., revenue increase, automation savings).  
- Optimize LinkedIn/GitHub with **keywords for remote skills**.  
- Tailor your resume and cover letter to remote-first companies.  

---

## 5. Negotiation Tips
- Always negotiate in **local-agnostic currency** (USD/EUR/GBP).  
- Highlight your unique **skills, speed, and reliability**.  
- Ask about **equity, bonuses, and flexible hours**.  
- Leverage multiple offers to maximize rates.  

---

## 6. Productivity & Remote Workflow
- **Communication:** Slack, Notion, Jira, Asana.  
- **Time Management:** Calendar blocking, Pomodoro, focus trackers.  
- **Automation:** GitHub Actions, CI/CD pipelines, scripts for repetitive tasks.  
- **Home Setup:** Quiet environment, dual monitors, stable internet.  

---
